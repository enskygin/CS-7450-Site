About Me

Katherine M. Ginensky is a graduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology.


Born and raised in Chicago, Illinois, Katherine attended the University of Illinois @ Chicago Urban Studies Program. After working at a local design and screen printing shop, she pursued urban planning, where she could apply her visual skills to describe and analyze cities.

Set to graduate from Georgia Tech with a Masters of Science in Urban Analytics in August 2024, she is excited to return to Chicago and put her new analysis and visualzation skills to use!

Research Interests







Katherine's research interests center around cities, especially how people interact with the spatial environment. Recently, her research has revolved around urban rodent issues.
